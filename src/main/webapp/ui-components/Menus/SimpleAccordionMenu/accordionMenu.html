What we do ?

We help our clients to develop custom software applications and assist them throughout the entire software development Life Cycle. This includes project management, systems analysis and design, development, implementation, training and application maintenance. After understanding your business model and needs, we follow a hybrid model of development, combining elements of Agile and Waterfall to keep you informed on every step and providing an accurate cost ahead of project start. We walk through prototypes, screen shots and working components of the application to give them clear picture of what is going to be built. This helps you to visualize final product at the beginning of project.
